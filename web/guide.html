Dashboard Navigation Guide

Metadata

This page provides an overview of sample metadata, including collection time, seasonality, and other essential details. Users can apply filters to explore subsets of the data interactively.

Region

The Region tab visualizes the spatial distribution of samples across various locations. Interactive maps and charts allow users to identify geographic patterns and compare different areas.

Sample

The Sample page allows users to inspect individual sample records, view their attributes, and understand how each sample contributes to broader trends observed in the dataset.

Phylogenetic Tree

This section illustrates evolutionary relationships between organisms found in the samples. Users can explore lineages, trace ancestries, and interact with a hierarchical tree structure.

Species

The Species tab highlights bacterial and viral species detected in the samples. Use filters and sorting tools to explore frequency, diversity, and species distribution across samples.
