Pathogen classification in this dashboard is based on established scientific databases and peer-reviewed literature to ensure accurate identification of potentially harmful microorganisms.

## Pathogen Sources

### Bacterial Pathogens

**NCBI Pathogen Detection Database**
Source: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pathogens/organisms

Comprehensive database of known bacterial pathogens based on clinical and epidemiological evidence, maintained by the National Center for Biotechnology Information.

**Bartlett et al.**
Source: https://doi.org/10.1099/mic.0.001269

Peer-reviewed literature providing evidence-based bacterial pathogen classifications and emerging pathogen information.

### Viral Pathogens

**Viral Zone Database**
Source: https://viralzone.expasy.org/678

Genus-level information for viral pathogens maintained by the Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics, offering taxonomic and functional information about viral families.

**International Committee on Taxonomy of Viruses (ICTV)**
Source: https://ictv.global/msl

Official taxonomic classification system for viruses providing species-level viral classifications through the Master Species List.
