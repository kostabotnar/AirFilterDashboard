This dashboard displays the culmination of over 800 air filters collected throughout the continental US during all four seasons from 2024-2025. The embedded Microsoft Power BI dashboard enables easy data browsing, visualization, and sorting. The raw next-generation sequencing data is available on the National Center for Biotechnology Information's Sequence Read Archive database. No biological or chemical materials were released into the air or environment. Data presented is a research and development analysis of air samples from the existing natural environment.
